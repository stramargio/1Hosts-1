Quickstart Guide

mini - Level 1: unblocks a number of ads & trackers for in-app rewards, anti-AdBlock, etc.
Lite - Level 2: set & forget, favours user experience (UX) over adblocking, balanced, recommended.
Pro - Level 3: prioritizes safety & privacy (adblocking) over UX e.g blocks graph.facebook.com.
Xtra - Level 4: extremly aggressive & restrictive. Do not use unless you know what you're doing!

Start with a Lite list first.
If you decide that Lite isn't for you, then move upwards or downwards the chain.

  • If Lite is too harsh for your taste, downgrade to mini.
  • If Lite does not block enough, upgrade to Pro.
  • Pro still not enough? Use Xtra.
